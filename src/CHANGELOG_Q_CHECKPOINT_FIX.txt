4. Polling starts immediately and uses serial timers to prevent overlapping async checks.
5. When a quarter break was missed, an old checkpoint is marked stale instead of firing during a later break.
6. Worker forwards --checkpoint 1/2/3 to Python.
7. Checkpoint 1 has a hard market allowlist: H1_TOTAL and TEAM_IT_H1 only. MATCH_TOTAL and TEAM_IT_MATCH are always blocked after Q1.
