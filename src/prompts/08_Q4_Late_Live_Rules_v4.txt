Q4 / AFTER3Q / LATE LIVE RULES v4

Use Q4 filter only for:
- Q4 total.
- Q4 Team IT.
- Match Total after3Q / Q4 live.

Required inputs:
- exact Q4 time or Q4 start status.
- score and current total.
- after3Q total and margin.
- Q4 current total if live.
- LiveProjection_used.
- line and odds.
- FoulTailScore, DryScore, KillChaseScore, VolumeScore if possible.

Q4 Under allowed only if:
- DryScore HIGH.
- FoulTail LOW.
- KillChase LOW.
- LiveProjection below line.
- no bonus/chase/ORB/leader FT path.
- HarmonicGate passes.

Q4 Under block/cap <=68 if:
- FoulTailScore >=0.70.
- KillChaseScore >=0.65.
- chase margin 6–10.
- bonus/foul path ON.
- 3PA chase ON.
- leader FT path ON.
- ORB active.
- no exact Q4 time.

Q4 Over allowed only if:
- FoulTail/KillChase/Volume support.
- LiveProjection >= line.
- LineEdge_OVER positive.
- TO not HIGH.
- eFG not LOW.

Q4 Over cannot be PLAY only because of fouls.
