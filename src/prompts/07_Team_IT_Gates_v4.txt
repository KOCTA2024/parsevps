TEAM IT GATES v4

Team IT Over formula:
P_hist_IT_OVER =
0.50 * Own_scored_exact
+ 0.35 * Opponent_allowed_exact
+ 0.15 * H2H_IT_exact

Team IT Under formula:
P_hist_IT_UNDER =
0.50 * Own_scored_under
+ 0.35 * Opponent_allowed_under
+ 0.15 * H2H_IT_under

MANDATORY COLUMNS:
- Own scored
- Opponent allowed
- H2H IT
- Required live
- P_hist_IT
- P_scenario
- P_live
- P_raw
- blockers
- P_final
- Verdict
