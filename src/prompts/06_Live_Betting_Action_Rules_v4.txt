LIVE BETTING ACTION RULES v4

CORE:
History = база.
Live = напрямок.
Stat-gate = підтвердження або блок.
P_final = verdict після caps/blockers/gates.

DO NOT USE SIMPLE PACE ONLY:
Projection_used requires:
1) Simple pace as info only.
2) Segment pace.
3) History baseline.
4) Scenario baseline.
5) Stat-adjusted projection if FGA/FTA/ORB/TO/Poss/eFG exist.
6) Conservative Projection_used.

HISTORY-SIDE BLOCK:
If history 90–100 Over but LiveProjection strongly below line:
- Over PASS / history-side blocked.
- Calculate Under live-side if real line exists.
If history 90–100 Under but LiveProjection strongly above line:
- Under PASS / history-side blocked.
- Calculate Over live-side if real line exists.

LIVE-SIDE EDGE MINIMUM:
Match Total: 10+ pts.
H1/H2 Total: 6–8+ pts.
Quarter Total: 5+ pts.
Team IT Match: 6–8+ pts.
Team IT Half/Quarter: 4–5+ pts.

STAT-GATE:
Over confirm needs 3/5:
FGA/Poss HIGH, FTA/FTr HIGH, ORB active, TO not HIGH, eFG not LOW / defense allows.
Under confirm needs 3/5:
FGA/Poss LOW, FTA/FTr LOW, ORB LOW, TO HIGH/empty, eFG LOW without high volume.

FAKE OVER BLOCK:
score/eFG HIGH but volume/FTA LOW.

FAKE UNDER BLOCK:
score/eFG LOW but FGA/Poss HIGH and ORB or FTA HIGH.

STAKE:
RISK ENTRY 68–72: 10–15% of live-limit.
LOW PLAY 73–74: 15%.
PLAY 75–79: 20–25%.
MAIN PLAY 80–84: 30–35%.
STRONG 85+: 40–50% only clean.
Stake is from daily/session live-limit, not full deposit.
